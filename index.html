ABOUT US

Welcome Message

Dear Kingdom citizen,

It is not by accident that you are here today — we believe that God occasioned your presence in today's service because He loves you and wants to bless you richly.

By His grace He gave us the mandate to restore the lost souls and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom for preservation through the preaching of the Word of Faith. We are committed to helping you discover the great future God has in store for you.

The Vision

In September 1979 after school hours, I engaged with Christian brethren about the end-time agenda. Suddenly I was taken up in the spirit and saw Jesus Christ seated upon a throne high and exalted. The whole sky was full of His Glory.

Royal Mandate

We are sent to restore lost souls and destinies into Christ's Kingdom through the preaching of the Word of Faith.

Mission Statement

1. Raise matured kingdom stars who will live Heaven on Earth.
2. Teach them through training programs how to use the Good News of His Kingdom to solve life challenges.
3. Infuse the vision and release them for expansion of His Kingdom.
