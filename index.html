About the Church

Kingdom Citizens Church International, located at Wole Iredele Street, Abiola, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria, is a family of believers passionate about the Kingdom of God. We are committed to raising Christians rooted in faith, walking in destiny, and living as heirs of the Kingdom.

Meet Our Pastors

Pastor Sam Balogun

Founder and Senior Pastor of Kingdom Citizens Church International, a visionary leader with a passion for faith, discipleship, and impacting lives for the glory of God. His leadership inspires many to walk boldly in their destiny.

Pastor Mrs Felicia Balogun

Co-founder and mother in faith, dedicated to nurturing believers, strengthening families, and fostering a culture of love and spiritual growth in the church community.

Pastor Wale Somoye

Resident Pastor and head of Osara branch, passionate about shepherding the flock, delivering the Word of God, and mentoring leaders to excellence.

Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi

Youth Pastor, devoted to empowering the next generation through teaching, mentorship, and instilling a passion for the Word of God in young believers.

Evangelist Daniel Onaleye

Head of the Choir and Evangelist, known for his inspiring worship leadership and evangelistic fervor, bringing souls into the Kingdom through music and the gospel message.
