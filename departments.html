Departments & Groups

Explore the ministries and groups of Kingdom Citizens Church International, each designed to nurture faith, service, and excellence in the body of Christ.

Founder & Senior Pastor, guiding the Church with divine wisdom and passion for the Kingdom.

Head of Choir & Evangelist, inspiring worship and guiding evangelistic missions.

Children's Department

Our Children's church is geared towards raising a new generation of children that will fear God and love humanity. A generation that will be solution providers to the problems of their society.

Teenagers' Department

This unit is committed in teaching our children who are between the ages of 13-18 Years as they gradually grow up to become young adults. Our focus also is to give them godly counsel and guidance to make right choices and decisions.

Youth Fellowship

This is the group of our young adults. Our main objective is to develop them in the wisdom and love of God by instilling in them biblical principles, encouraging them to be students of the Word and teaching them how to handle life challenges using the Word. Meetings: 2nd Friday monthly, 11 pm–4 am.

Women’s Fellowship (Royal Pillars)

We are committed to the successful family life of our members. Women gather every 2nd Saturday of the month at 9 am for fellowship and prayer. Royal Sisters: Under 45 yrs. Royal Pillars: 45 yrs and above.

Men’s Fellowship

This group is twofold: Business Men Fellowship and Men of Issachar. Our aim is to raise kingdom-minded men who bring heaven to their homes and lead exemplary lives. Meetings: every Saturday 4:30 pm for business fellowship and general prayer.

Kingdom Citizens Voices (Choir)

We give high premium to praise and worship of God. Our praise team is gifted and devoted to fulfilling Ps. 100:4-5 and Ps. 22:3. They meet every Saturday at 4 pm.

Ushering Group

This team maintains order within the church. They are the “physical angels” welcoming everyone to service.

Royal Security Unit

The kingdom law-enforcement agents ensuring cars are parked orderly and crowds are under check.

Evangelism Team

Fulfilling the Great Commission (Matt. 28:19-20), this team wins souls, follows up newcomers, and organizes crusades and prayer meetings.

Burden Bearers (Prayer Group)

Dynamic individuals devoted to praying for the church, pastors, and revival. Meetings: every Tuesday 5:30 pm and Sunday 6:30–7:40 am.

Sanctuary Keepers & Decorators

This team keeps the church building and environment neat. As they care for God’s house, God cares for them (Ex. 23:25-26).

Media Unit (ICT Group)

Handles visual/audio production, publications, and internet outreach to spread the gospel of our Lord Jesus.

Prayer Centre – Home Fellowship

The church in homes: fostering pastoral relationships, extending love and care as in Acts 2:46. A harvest basket for new souls.
