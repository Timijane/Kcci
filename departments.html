Departments & Ministers

Explore the dynamic ministries and dedicated ministers of Kingdom Citizens Church International. Each department is uniquely designed to nurture faith, develop spiritual gifts, and advance God's kingdom through specialized ministries and passionate leadership.

Pastoral Leadership

Our pastoral team provides visionary leadership, spiritual guidance, and dedicated service to shepherd the flock of God at Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L.

Pastor Samuel Abiodun Balogun

Founder & Senior Pastor

Visionary leader and founder of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L with over 25 years of ministerial experience, dedicated to raising kingdom citizens who transform their world through faith and action.

Pastor Samuel Abiodun Balogun is a dynamic apostolic leader called by God to establish kingdom principles in the lives of believers. As the founder and senior pastor of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, he has pioneered a ministry that emphasizes royal priesthood, divine inheritance, and kingdom citizenship.

He is also the founder of Seed Royal Bible Church and the International School of Kingdom Citizenship, through which he has trained and mentored hundreds of ministers and church planters. With a doctorate in Divinity and extensive theological training, Pastor Balogun combines deep biblical scholarship with practical kingdom application.

His ministry is characterized by prophetic accuracy, apostolic vision, and a relentless commitment to soul-winning and discipleship. He has authored several books including "The Kingdom Citizen's Mandate" and "Walking in Royal Authority." Under his leadership, Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L has grown from a home fellowship to a multinational ministry with multiple branches and significant community impact.

Pastor Balogun is married to Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun and they are blessed with children who are actively involved in ministry. His life motto is: "Every believer is a kingdom citizen with royal authority and divine responsibility."

Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun

Co-founder & Minister of Helps

A retired Deputy Director of Nursing Services (DDNS) who brings administrative excellence and compassionate care to ministry, supporting the vision with wisdom and dedication.

Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun is a woman of exceptional grace, wisdom, and spiritual depth. With a distinguished career as a Deputy Director of Nursing Services (DDNS) spanning over 30 years, she brings professional excellence, administrative acumen, and compassionate care to her ministerial calling.

As the co-founder of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, she provides crucial support to the senior pastor while leading the women's ministry, counseling department, and hospitality teams. Her nursing background uniquely equips her for the ministry of healing, both physical and emotional, as she oversees prayer and counseling sessions.

She is the founder of "Royal Women of Purpose," a ministry that empowers women to discover their God-given identity and fulfill their divine purpose. Through monthly meetings, annual retreats, and mentorship programs, she has impacted hundreds of women across different generations.

Pastor Mrs. Balogun holds a Master's degree in Public Administration and several certifications in Christian counseling. She is a sought-after speaker at women's conferences and marriage seminars. Her teaching series "The Proverbs 31 Woman in Today's World" has transformed countless homes and marriages.

A devoted mother and grandmother, she exemplifies balanced living—successfully managing family, career, and ministry while maintaining a vibrant prayer life. Her favorite scripture is Proverbs 31:25: "She is clothed with strength and dignity, and she laughs without fear of the future."

Pastor Wale Somoye

Resident Pastor (Osara Branch)

Dedicated shepherd overseeing the Osara branch with commitment to discipleship, community outreach, and pastoral care for congregation members.

Pastor Wale Somoye serves as the resident pastor of the Osara branch of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, providing dedicated pastoral oversight and spiritual leadership to the congregation. With over 15 years of ministerial experience, he brings a balanced approach to teaching, discipleship, and community engagement.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Theology and a Master's in Christian Leadership, complemented by specialized training in church administration and small group dynamics. Under his leadership, the Osara branch has experienced significant growth in membership and community impact through various outreach programs.

Pastor Somoye is particularly gifted in expository teaching and practical application of biblical principles to everyday life. He oversees the church's discipleship program "Kingdom Foundations" which has trained over 300 believers in foundational Christian doctrines and kingdom principles.

He is married to Deaconess Grace Somoye, and together they lead the marriage enrichment ministry "Covenant Keepers." Their testimony of God's faithfulness in their 18-year marriage inspires many couples in the congregation.

Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi

Youth Pastor & Campus Minister

Dynamic youth minister passionate about raising a generation of young believers who walk in purpose, power, and spiritual authority.

Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi, affectionately called "PK" by the youth, leads with contagious energy and deep spiritual insight. As the Youth Pastor of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, he oversees all youth and campus ministries with a vision to raise a generation of kingdom-minded young leaders.

With a background in Mass Communication and specialized training in youth ministry from the International Youth Ministry Institute, Pastor Peter connects authentically with young people while challenging them to higher spiritual standards. He pioneered the "NextGen Kingdom Leaders" program that has produced many youth leaders now serving in various capacities.

He also serves as the Campus Minister at the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA), where he leads the Kingdom Citizens Campus Fellowship. Under his leadership, the campus ministry has grown to become one of the most vibrant Christian groups on campus, known for impactful evangelism and discipleship.

Pastor Peter is a gifted communicator and worship leader. He has released several worship songs and leads the youth worship team "Kingdom Youth Voices." His annual youth conference "Awaken Generation" attracts hundreds of young people from across the state.

Evangelist Daniel Onaleye

Head of Choir & Evangelism Director

Anointed worship leader and passionate evangelist who combines musical excellence with fervent soul-winning to advance God's kingdom.

Evangelist Daniel Onaleye is a multi-talented minister who combines musical excellence with evangelistic fervor. As the Head of the Kingdom Citizens Voices (Choir), he leads worship with anointing and excellence that ushers congregations into God's presence.

With formal training in Music from the Nigerian Institute of Music and over 20 years of worship ministry experience, Evangelist Daniel has developed the choir into a proficient worship team that ministers both locally and nationally. He has composed and arranged numerous worship songs that have become church favorites.

In his role as Evangelism Director, he coordinates weekly outreach programs, neighborhood evangelism, and special crusades. Under his leadership, the church's evangelism team has recorded over 5,000 decisions for Christ in the past three years. He pioneered the "Every Member Evangelist" training program that equips ordinary believers for effective soul-winning.

Evangelist Daniel is also a skilled music teacher who trains choir members in vocal techniques, harmony, and musical theory. He believes that "worship is warfare" and that "every convert should become a worshipper."

He is married to Sister Joy Onaleye, who serves as the assistant choir director. Together, they are raising their three children to love God and serve in ministry.

Ministry Departments

Our specialized departments work together to build a strong, vibrant church community where every member can grow, serve, and fulfill their God-given purpose.

Children's Department

Raising Kingdom Kids for Divine Destiny

Our Children's church is dedicated to raising a godly generation of children who fear God, love humanity, and walk in their divine identity as kingdom citizens from an early age.

The Children's Department, also known as "Kingdom Kids Academy," provides age-appropriate biblical teaching, worship, and discipleship for children ages 2-12. We believe children are not just the church of tomorrow but vital members of the church today.

Our curriculum is designed to teach children foundational biblical truths through interactive lessons, worship, drama, arts and crafts, and memory verses. Each class is staffed by trained, background-checked teachers who are passionate about children's spiritual development.

Age Groups

Nursery (2-4), Pre-school (5-7), Juniors (8-10), Pre-teens (11-12)

Weekly Programs

Sunday School, Children's Church, Wednesday Bible Club, Vacation Bible School

Special Events

Annual Children's Convention, Easter Egg Hunt, Christmas Pageant, Back-to-School Blessing

Our vision is to see every child develop a personal relationship with Jesus, understand their identity in Christ, and begin to operate in their spiritual gifts. We partner with parents through regular parenting seminars and take-home materials to reinforce biblical values at home.

Teenagers' Department

Equipping Teens for Purposeful Living

This unit is committed to guiding teenagers (ages 13-18) through the challenging transition to young adulthood with biblical principles, mentorship, and peer support.

The Teenagers' Department, called "Kingdom Teens," creates a safe, engaging environment where teenagers can explore faith, ask tough questions, and develop their God-given identity during these formative years.

We address relevant issues facing today's teens including peer pressure, identity, relationships, social media, academics, and career choices from a biblical perspective. Our approach combines teaching, small group discussions, mentorship, and fun activities.

Core Programs

Sunday Teen Service, Friday Night Live (FNL), Small Groups, Leadership Training

Annual Events

Teen Camp, Career Discovery Day, Youth Evangelism Outreach, Talent Showcase

Development Areas

Spiritual growth, Leadership skills, Academic excellence, Social responsibility

Each teen is paired with a caring adult mentor who provides guidance, prayer support, and accountability. We also offer specialized programs for teen guys ("Young Warriors") and teen girls ("Daughter of Zion") to address gender-specific issues.

Our goal is to produce teenagers who are spiritually grounded, academically excellent, socially responsible, and prepared to make a positive impact in their world.

Youth Fellowship

Young Adults Walking in Wisdom and Purpose

A vibrant community of young adults (ages 19-35) committed to developing in God's wisdom, building authentic relationships, and impacting their spheres of influence.

The Youth Fellowship, known as "Kingdom Young Adults (KYA)," provides spiritual nourishment, fellowship, and practical support for single and married young adults navigating early adulthood, careers, relationships, and family life.

We address real-life issues including career development, financial stewardship, relationships and marriage preparation, spiritual disciplines, and marketplace ministry. Our meetings feature relevant teaching, interactive workshops, and open discussions.

Meeting Schedule

Sunday Service (9am), Thursday Bible Study (6pm), Monthly Socials, Quarterly Retreats

Specialized Groups

Career Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Students, Newly Marrieds, Singles

Skill Development

Leadership training, Financial literacy, Career counseling, Relationship coaching

We believe young adults are crucial to the advancement of God's kingdom in this generation. Through our "Kingdom Impact" initiative, members serve in various church ministries and community projects. Our annual "Young Leaders Summit" equips youth for greater influence and service.

Whether you're a student, professional, entrepreneur, or stay-at-home parent, you'll find a welcoming community where you can grow spiritually and build meaningful relationships.

Women's Fellowship (Royal Pillars)

Women of Strength, Wisdom, and Grace

Committed to the spiritual, emotional, and practical empowerment of women at every life stage through biblical teaching, fellowship, and supportive relationships.

The Royal Pillars Women's Fellowship is a vibrant community where women find spiritual nourishment, authentic friendships, and practical support for every season of life. Led by Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun, this ministry addresses the unique needs and challenges women face today.

Our monthly meetings feature dynamic teaching on topics such as spiritual growth, marriage enrichment, parenting, health and wellness, financial stewardship, and personal development. We also offer specialized support groups for singles, married women, mothers, widows, and career women.

Monthly Programs

First Saturday Fellowship, Weekly Prayer Meetings, Monthly Bible Study, Quarterly Seminars

Annual Events

Women's Conference, Mother-Daughter Banquet, Widows' Outreach, Skills Acquisition Workshop

Support Ministries

Prayer Warriors, Hospitality Team, Benevolence Committee, Mentorship Program

Through our "Sister-to-Sister" mentorship program, younger women are paired with mature women for guidance and discipleship. Our "Royal Business Women" network supports female entrepreneurs with training, networking, and resources.

We believe every woman is called to be a pillar of strength in her family, church, and community. Whether you're single, married, divorced, or widowed, you'll find acceptance, support, and opportunities to grow and serve.

Men's Fellowship

Men of Character, Courage, and Conviction

Equipping men to fulfill their God-given roles as spiritual leaders, faithful husbands, loving fathers, and influential kingdom agents in society.

The Men's Fellowship at Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L consists of two complementary groups: the Business Men Fellowship and the Men of Issachar. Together, they provide spiritual, professional, and personal development for men of all ages and backgrounds.

The Business Men Fellowship focuses on integrating faith and work, providing networking opportunities, business mentoring, and financial wisdom from biblical principles. The Men of Issachar emphasizes spiritual leadership, family stewardship, and understanding the times (1 Chronicles 12:32).

Business Men Fellowship

Monthly breakfast meetings, Business networking, Entrepreneur training, Financial stewardship workshops

Men of Issachar

Monthly prayer breakfast, Leadership training, Family enrichment seminars, Community service projects

Joint Activities

Annual Men's Retreat, Father-Son Outing, Sports Fellowship, Skill Development Workshops

Our "Iron Sharpens Iron" mentorship program pairs younger men with mature Christian men for discipleship and accountability. Through our "Man Up" initiative, we train men to take spiritual responsibility in their homes, workplaces, and communities.

We believe godly men are catalysts for transformation in families, churches, and nations. Join us as we pursue biblical masculinity, authentic brotherhood, and kingdom impact.

Kingdom Citizens Voices (Choir)

Ministering God's Presence Through Excellence in Worship

A dedicated team of worshippers who lead the congregation into God's presence through anointed music, vocals, and instruments, giving high premium to praise and worship.

The Kingdom Citizens Voices, under the leadership of Evangelist Daniel Onaleye, is more than just a choir—it's a worship ministry dedicated to ushering God's people into His presence through Spirit-led, excellent worship.

Our choir consists of vocalists, instrumentalists, and technical crew who serve across multiple services and special events. We believe worship is both a weapon of spiritual warfare and a vehicle for divine encounter.

Ministry Teams

Main Choir, Youth Choir, Instrumentalists, Technical Crew, Worship Leaders

Rehearsal Schedule

Tuesday (Sectionals), Thursday (Full Choir), Saturday (Full Rehearsal with Band)

Musical Training

Vocal techniques, Harmony training, Sight reading, Instrument proficiency

We regularly release original worship songs and have produced several albums that have blessed congregations beyond our local church. Our annual "Night of Worship" event draws worshippers from across the region for an unforgettable encounter with God.

Membership in the choir requires commitment to spiritual growth, musical development, and team unity. Auditions are held quarterly, and new members undergo a training period before joining the main choir.

Ushering Group

Creating a Worship-Friendly Atmosphere with Excellence

The frontline ministry team that maintains order, welcomes worshippers, assists with seating, distributes materials, and ensures a conducive atmosphere for worship.

The Ushering Group serves as the "first face" of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, creating welcoming, orderly, and worship-friendly environments for all services and events. We understand that effective ushering enhances the worship experience and facilitates ministry.

Our ushers are trained in hospitality, crowd management, basic first aid, emergency response, and conflict resolution. They serve not only as greeters but as ministers of helps who discreetly attend to needs during services.

Service Areas

Greeting & Welcome, Seating Assistance, Offering Collection, Communion Service, Special Events

Training Programs

Monthly training sessions, Quarterly workshops, Annual retreat, Continuous evaluation

Specialized Teams

Welcome Team, Seating Team, Offering Team, First Aid Team, Emergency Response Team

We believe every usher is an ambassador of Christ's love and a facilitator of worship. Our motto is "Service with a Smile, Excellence in Execution." Through regular training and team building, we strive for continuous improvement in our service.

New ushers undergo a comprehensive training program covering biblical principles of hospitality, practical ushering skills, and church policies. Join us in creating memorable first impressions and seamless worship experiences.

Royal Security Unit

Ensuring Safety and Order for Worry-Free Worship

The kingdom law-enforcement agents dedicated to maintaining security, orderly parking, and overall safety during all church activities and events.

The Royal Security Unit is a team of trained volunteers committed to providing a safe, secure environment for worship and ministry activities. We understand that people cannot worship freely if they feel unsafe, so we work diligently to prevent and respond to security concerns.

Our responsibilities include parking lot management, building security during services, crowd control at events, emergency response coordination, and liaison with local law enforcement when necessary.

Security Zones

Parking Lot Security, Building Perimeter, Children's Area, Main Auditorium, Special Events

Training & Equipment

CPR/First Aid certified, Conflict de-escalation, Emergency procedures, Communication systems

Special Protocols

Missing child procedure, Medical emergency response, Severe weather plan, Fire evacuation

All security team members undergo background checks and receive regular training in safety protocols, emergency response, and professional conduct. We work closely with the ushering team and church leadership to ensure comprehensive coverage.

Our approach combines vigilance with hospitality—we aim to be both watchful and welcoming. By maintaining order and safety, we create an atmosphere where worshippers can focus on encountering God without distraction or concern.

Evangelism Team

Fulfilling the Great Commission Through Strategic Outreach

Passionate soul-winners committed to spreading the gospel through personal evangelism, neighborhood outreach, crusades, and innovative evangelistic strategies.

The Evangelism Team, under the leadership of Evangelist Daniel Onaleye, is dedicated to fulfilling Matthew 28:19-20—the Great Commission. We believe evangelism is not just an activity but the heartbeat of the church and every believer's responsibility.

Our team employs multiple approaches to reach people with the gospel: door-to-door evangelism, street preaching, marketplace ministry, literature distribution, social media evangelism, and special outreach events.

Weekly Outreach

Saturday Evangelism (10am), Sunday Follow-up Visitation, Wednesday Prayer Walk, Friday Youth Outreach

Training Programs

Monthly Evangelism Training, Quarterly Soul-winning Seminar, Annual Evangelism Conference

Special Ministries

Prison Ministry, Hospital Visitation, Campus Evangelism, Community Service Projects

We track our outreach efforts and follow up diligently with new converts through our "New Believers' Care" program. Each new believer is assigned a mentor who disciples them for the first 90 days after conversion.

Our annual "Harvest Crusade" brings together multiple churches for city-wide evangelism. Through partnerships with other ministries, we've seen thousands come to Christ and integrated into local churches.

We believe every member is an evangelist. Through our "Every Member Evangelist" training, we equip ordinary believers with simple, effective tools for sharing their faith in everyday contexts.

Burden Bearers (Prayer Group)

Interceding for Breakthrough, Revival, and Divine Intervention

Dynamic individuals devoted to praying for the church, pastors, members, community, and nations—standing in the gap through fervent, strategic intercession.

The Burden Bearers Prayer Group is the spiritual engine room of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L. We understand that prayer is not preparation for the work—it is the work. Through consistent, fervent intercession, we contend for God's purposes in our church, city, and nation.

Our prayer meetings are characterized by biblical praying, prophetic declarations, worship-infused intercession, and travail for breakthrough. We pray according to specific themes each month, aligning our prayers with the church's vision and current needs.

Prayer Schedule

Monday-Friday (6am-7am), Wednesday (5pm-6pm), Friday All-Night (Monthly), Sunday Pre-service (8am)

Prayer Focus Areas

Church Leadership, Membership Needs, Community Transformation, National Issues, World Missions

Special Prayer Initiatives

21-Day Fast (January), 40-Day Lenten Fast, Prayer Walks, Prayer Chains, Emergency Prayer Network

We maintain a 24/7 prayer chain for emergency prayer requests and operate a prayer hotline for those needing immediate intercession. Our prayer warriors are also available after services for personal prayer ministry.

Through our "School of Prayer" training, we teach members how to pray effectively according to biblical patterns. We believe every believer is called to a life of prayer, and we equip them to fulfill this calling.

Join us as we build a wall of prayer around our church, community, and nation. Together, we can move the hand of God through believing, persistent prayer.

Sanctuary Keepers & Decorators

Maintaining Beauty and Order in God's House

Dedicated teams that keep the church building and environment clean, beautiful, and welcoming—creating an atmosphere conducive to worship and ministry.

The Sanctuary Keepers & Decorators understand that the physical environment of worship matters. We believe God deserves our best in every area, including the cleanliness, maintenance, and aesthetic presentation of His house.

Our ministry is divided into two complementary teams: the Sanctuary Keepers focus on cleaning and maintenance, while the Decorators focus on aesthetic enhancement and seasonal decorations.

Sanctuary Keepers

Weekly cleaning teams, Building maintenance, Grounds keeping, Setup/teardown for events

Decorators Team

Seasonal decorations, Special event setups, Floral arrangements, Visual design for services

Maintenance Areas

Sanctuary, Classrooms, Offices, Restrooms, Grounds, Parking areas

We operate on a rotating schedule with teams serving weekly, monthly, and for special events. Our "Adopt-a-Space" program allows individuals or families to take responsibility for specific areas of the church facility.

Through careful attention to detail, we create environments that honor God and enhance worship. Whether it's ensuring spotless facilities or creating beautiful seasonal displays, we serve joyfully as unto the Lord.

We believe that excellence in maintaining God's house reflects our reverence for Him and creates a welcoming atmosphere for both members and visitors. Join us in this practical but vital ministry of helps.

Media Unit (ICT Group)

Amplifying God's Message Through Technology and Creativity

Handles visual/audio production, publications, and internet outreach—leveraging technology to spread the gospel and enhance ministry effectiveness.

The Media Unit, also known as the ICT Group, serves as the technological arm of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L. We harness the power of media and technology to amplify the gospel, document ministry activities, and enhance worship experiences.

Our team consists of photographers, videographers, sound engineers, graphic designers, web developers, social media managers, and livestream technicians. We work behind the scenes to ensure that both in-person and online attendees have excellent experiences.

Production Areas

Audio Engineering, Video Production, Livestream Broadcasting, Photography, Graphic Design

Digital Platforms

Church Website, Mobile App, Social Media, Podcast, YouTube Channel, Email Newsletter

Publications

Weekly Bulletin, Monthly Magazine, Event Programs, Promotional Materials, Ministry Resources

We provide comprehensive coverage of all services and events, producing high-quality recordings for distribution and archival purposes. Our livestream ministry reaches thousands of viewers weekly across multiple platforms.

Through our "Media Academy" training program, we equip members with technical skills in various media disciplines. We believe every church should harness technology for kingdom advancement, and we're committed to excellence in this area.

Whether you're tech-savvy or just willing to learn, there's a place for you in the Media Unit. Join us as we use our gifts and skills to spread the gospel in the digital age.

Prayer Centre – Home Fellowship

The Church in Homes: Building Community Beyond Sunday

Neighborhood-based small groups that foster pastoral relationships, extend love and care, and provide midweek spiritual nourishment in homes across the community.

The Prayer Centre – Home Fellowship ministry extends the church beyond the four walls of the building into homes across our community. We believe that authentic Christian community happens best in smaller settings where people can connect, care for one another, and grow together.

Our home fellowships meet weekly in various neighborhoods, providing convenient locations for midweek Bible study, prayer, fellowship, and pastoral care. Each group is led by trained leaders who shepherd their members with love and diligence.

Meeting Structure

Weekly gatherings (various days), Bible study discussion, Prayer time, Fellowship & refreshments

Group Types

Geographic (by neighborhood), Demographic (men, women, couples, singles), Language groups

Care Ministries

Member visitation, Crisis support, New member integration, Prayer support, Practical helps

Home fellowships serve as the primary care structure for our congregation. Leaders are trained to identify needs, provide pastoral support, and connect members with appropriate church resources. They also serve as evangelistic outreach points in their communities.

We offer specialized training for home fellowship leaders through our "Shepherd's Heart" program, covering topics such as facilitating group discussions, providing basic counseling, leading prayer, and identifying member needs.

Whether you're new to the church or a long-time member, joining a home fellowship will deepen your connections and accelerate your spiritual growth. Find your home away from home today!
