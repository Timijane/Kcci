About Seed Royal School

Seed Royal School is the educational arm of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, providing excellent Christian education from crèche to secondary school levels.

Age Range

6 months - 16 years

Curriculum

Nigerian & British Curriculum

Class Size

15-20 students per class

Foundation

Christian values & principles

Seed Royal Bible Study Program

Our comprehensive Bible study curriculum is integrated into daily school activities, helping students develop a strong spiritual foundation alongside academic excellence.

Crèche Level

6 months - 2 years

Introduction to Bible stories through songs, pictures, and simple prayers

Focus: God's love & creation

Nursery Level

2 - 4 years

Basic Bible stories, Christian values, and character development

Focus: Obedience & kindness

Primary Level

5 - 11 years

Systematic Bible study, memory verses, and Christian leadership principles

Focus: Faith & service

Secondary Level

11 - 17 years

Advanced Bible study, apologetics, Christian worldview, and discipleship

Focus: Kingdom citizenship
