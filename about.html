ABOUT US

CHURCH HISTORY

In September 1979 after the tedious school hours, I engaged myself discussing with some Christian Brethren about the end time agenda of God. Particularly our discussion was centered on the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ. A few minutes after this discussion, I left them and I was alone in a room when suddenly I was taken up in the spirit, I lifted up my eyes and looked. Behold I saw Jesus Christ seated upon a throne high and exalted. The whole sky was full of His Glory.

The Son of God appeared in the clouds of Heaven in power and great glory. I saw thousands of His Angels around the throne sent through the whole Earth. While later I saw these Angels cross-examining both slave and free according to each person's deeds. Those found guilty were seen burning inside hot furnaces of fire.

I saw a square shaped glass house by the right hand side of the throne of Judgment reserved for the blameless. When it got to my turn to be cross examined, I was found blameless and I was ushered into this square shaped glass house.

As I took the last step to enter into this glorious building, I was transformed; the whole apparel on me changed to a golden-linen material. Though few of us could make it into this glass house full of everything you need here on Earth — an Eden of no pain, no sorrow, no night, no death, no heat, no hunger. This is Heaven's Kingdom on the Earth. (Rev. 21:1-End)

THE VISION

Our vision is to raise and nurture a generation of Kingdom Citizens who will live Heaven on Earth. We aim to restore lost souls and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom through the preaching of the Word of Faith. This vision drives every activity in the church and ensures that our members live a life empowered by faith and divine purpose.

ROYAL MANDATE

We are sent to restore the lost souls and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom for preservation through the preaching of the Word of Faith. Our mandate is to guide, train, and equip every believer to fulfill their divine purpose and live victoriously in Christ.

MISSION STATEMENT

Why are we sent?

  • To raise with God a family of matured kingdom stars who will live Heaven practically on the Earth, getting them ready for the second coming of the KING.
  • To teach them through training programmes how to use the Good News of His Kingdom to solve life challenges in marriage, career, profession, business, finances, moral, and spiritual matters.
  • To infuse the vision into them and release them for the expansion of His Kingdom on the Earth.
