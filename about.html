Our Journey Since 1979

1979

The Divine Revelation

In September 1979 after tedious school hours, I engaged myself discussing with some Christian Brethren about the end time agenda of God. Our discussion centered on the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

A few minutes after this discussion, I left them and was alone in a room when suddenly I was taken up in the spirit. I lifted up my eyes and looked. Behold I saw Jesus Christ seated upon a throne high and exalted. The whole sky was full of His Glory.

1979

The Vision of Judgment

The Son of God appeared in the clouds of Heaven in power and great glory. I saw thousands of His Angels around the throne sent through the whole Earth. While later I saw these Angels cross-examining both slave and free according to each person's deeds. Those found guilty were seen burning inside hot furnaces of fire.

I saw a square shaped glass house by the right hand side of the throne of Judgment reserved for the blameless.

1979

The Divine Transformation

When it got to my turn to be cross examined, I was found blameless and I was ushered into this square shaped glass house. As I took the last step to enter into this glorious building, I was transformed; the whole apparel on me changed to a golden-linen material.

Though few of us could make it into this glass house full of everything you need here on Earth—an Eden of no pain, no sorrow, no night, no death, no heat, no hunger. This is Heaven's Kingdom on the Earth. (Rev. 21:1–End)

Our Purpose & Calling

Royal Mandate

We are sent to restore the lost soul and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom for preservation through the preaching of the Word of Faith.

Our mandate is to raise a royal priesthood, a holy nation, a people belonging to God, who will declare His praises and extend His kingdom on earth.

Mission Statement

  • To raise with God a family of matured kingdom stars who will live Heaven practically on the Earth, getting them ready for the second coming of the KING.
  • To teach them through training programmes how to use the Good News of His Kingdom to solve life challenges in marriage, career, profession, business, finances, moral and spiritual matters.
  • To infuse the vision into them and release them for the expansion of His Kingdom on the Earth.
  • To establish kingdom citizens in every sphere of society who will transform their world through kingdom principles.

The Vision

Our vision is to raise and nurture a generation of Kingdom Citizens who will live Heaven on Earth. We aim to restore lost souls and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom through the preaching of the Word of Faith, ensuring our members live a life empowered by faith and divine purpose.

We envision communities transformed by kingdom values, where every citizen lives out their royal identity and purpose in Christ, impacting generations to come.

Our Core Values

Christ-Centered

Jesus Christ is the foundation, center, and goal of everything we do. We exist to glorify Him and make Him known.

Bible-Based

God's Word is our final authority. We teach the whole counsel of Scripture, applying it to every area of life.

Prayer-Driven

We are a house of prayer for all nations. Prayer is our first response, not our last resort.

Kingdom Community

We value authentic relationships and spiritual family. We grow together, serve together, and advance God's kingdom together.

Compassionate Service

We demonstrate Christ's love through practical acts of service, meeting both spiritual and physical needs.

Generational Impact

We invest in the next generation, raising kingdom citizens who will continue the legacy of faith.
