Youth Section

The Youth Section empowers young believers to grow in faith, leadership, and service. Through mentorship, teaching, and vibrant fellowship, the youth are equipped to become influential ambassadors for Christ in their schools, communities, and beyond.

Choir Section

The Choir Section is the heartbeat of worship at Kingdom Citizens Church. Members cultivate their talents to lead the congregation in uplifting songs, glorifying God, and inspiring worship that transcends hearts and souls.

Royal Pillars

The Royal Pillars are dedicated men and women committed to prayer, mentorship, and community service. They stand firm in faith, offering guidance, spiritual support, and wisdom to strengthen the Church family.

Men of Issachar

This group seeks spiritual insight and understanding, discerning the times and applying God’s Word to life. They are committed to prayer, study, and leading the Church in wise counsel and faithful service.

Children Section

The Children Section nurtures the next generation in faith, love, and knowledge of God’s Word. Through engaging teaching, fun activities, and spiritual mentorship, children are empowered to grow as heirs of the Kingdom.

Media Department

The Media Department captures, creates, and shares the message of the Church across platforms. With creativity and dedication, they ensure the vision and mission of the Church reach hearts locally and globally.
