Our Locations

Find Kingdom Citizens Church Near You

Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L is growing to serve you better! We currently have two vibrant worship centers in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. Whether you're in the Abiola area or around Osara Obantoko, there's a Kingdom Citizens Church near you.

Our doors are always open to welcome you into our family. Each location maintains the same standard of worship, teaching, and fellowship that has become our hallmark. Join us at either of our centers and experience the transforming power of God's presence.
