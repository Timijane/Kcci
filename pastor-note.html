Welcome

Discover Your Identity as a Kingdom Citizen

Dear Kingdom Citizen,

It is not by accident that you are here today; we believe that God occasioned your presence because He loves you and wants to bless you richly. The entire congregation of Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L warmly welcomes you.

By His grace He gave us the mandate to restore the lost souls and destinies back into Christ's Kingdom for preservation through the preaching of the word of faith. Therefore we are committed to helping you discover the great future that God has in store for you and ultimately recover what the devil has stolen from you.

Who is a Kingdom Citizen?

By creation man was made in the class of God. Adam in the Garden of Eden by God's wisdom named all the animals one by one, but when he fell his wisdom was corrupted. It did away with the nature of God for the nature of the devil in sin and wickedness. Every mortal being shared this nature with Adam by natural birth (John 3:5–6; Romans 3:23).

But thank God for Jesus who died for you and wants to spend eternity with you. You have a part to play: accept Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior.

Prayer of Salvation

Lord Jesus I am a sinner, forgive me my sin. I know you died for me, I know you rose on the third day. Lord Jesus wash me with your blood, come into my heart. I receive you today as my Lord and Savior. Write my name in the Book of Life. I am born again, I am now a Kingdom citizen. I am no longer an earthly being but a heavenly being.

If you prayed this prayer, you are now born again! Welcome to God's family!

Your Kingdom Identity

Your Rights as a Kingdom Citizen

1

You Are a New Creation

2 Corinthians 5:17 - "Therefore if any man be in Christ he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new."

As you have given your life to Christ, you become a new citizen of a new kingdom and a member of God's family on Earth. You possess Heaven's green card and are entitled to the second resurrection. The second death has no power over you (1 John 4:5).

2

Your Sins Are Forgiven

Romans 8:1 - "There is therefore now no condemnation to those who are in Christ Jesus."

Salvation is a product of grace, not of works. Grace grants you justification in the sight of God. It is important for you to know that by the blood of Jesus your sins are forgiven. You are thoroughly made clean and perfect (2 Corinthians 5:21).

3

Free from Curses

Galatians 3:13 - "Christ has redeemed us from the curse of the law, being made a curse for us: for it is written, Cursed is every one that hangs on a tree."

You are now free from every curse - poverty, failure, sickness, barrenness. Family curses or demonic agents can't place any curse on you, nor can any charm have effect on you (Numbers 23:23, Ephesians 23:25-26, Isaiah 54:17, Luke 10:19).

4

Satan Is Under Your Feet

Ephesians 1:21 - "And hath raised us up together, and made us sit together in heavenly places in Christ Jesus: Far above all principality, and power, and might, and dominion, and every name that is named, not only in this world, but also in that which is to come."

As a heavenly citizen you are seated together with Jesus in heavenly places far above witches, wizards, occult powers, and Satan himself. Your new status makes you a god over the devil and his evil works (Psalm 82:5-6, John 10:34–35).

Your Duties as a Kingdom Citizen

1

You Need Knowledge

Hosea 4:6 - "My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge."

You are now born into the world of God (by salvation), the kingdom of God. So you need to know the way things work in this new Kingdom so that you are not a misfit or a stumbling block to others (John 8:32).

2

Attend Foundation Class

Rudiments of Christian Faith - This class holds every Saturday morning @8am. This is a discipleship school for beginners in the Kingdom. We teach believers the foundational truths of the Christian faith.

Also attend Royal Crown Bible School for deeper biblical understanding.

3

Study God's Word

2 Timothy 2:15 - "Study to show thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth."

Everything you need to know about God and His ways and about your new life is in the Bible. Read your Bible more than any book in this world. The more you read, the more you know about God and His Kingdom, and the more you know about your rights and privileges as a Kingdom Citizen.

4

Read Anointed Christian Books

Daniel 9:2 - "In the first year of his reign I Daniel understood by books..."

Every anointed Christian book comes with revelation of hidden truth in the Bible. There are books written on marriage, business, Christian character, etc. Boost your faith by laying hold on any and read.

Essential Practices

Don't Miss Church Programs

Hebrews 10:25 - "Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is..." Church is where kingdom citizens are trained and nurtured through the undiluted word of truth under the anointing of the Holy Spirit.

Get Involved

Do not play with Church. Whenever there is a program in Church, don't prefer to be anywhere else. Get involved in church activities and make up your mind to join us every Sunday.

Prayer Life

Develop a consistent prayer life. Prayer is your communication line with God. As a Kingdom Citizen, you have direct access to the throne of grace (Hebrews 4:16).

From the Founder's Desk

Welcome to the family of God! As you begin this new journey as a Kingdom Citizen, remember that you are now part of something eternal. You have been transferred from the kingdom of darkness into the Kingdom of God's dear Son (Colossians 1:13).

Walk in your new identity, exercise your rights, fulfill your duties, and watch as God transforms every area of your life. We are here to help you grow and mature in your faith.

You're welcome,

Pastor Sam Balogun

Founder, Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L
