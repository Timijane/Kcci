Pastor Balogun’s Desk

Who is a Kingdom Citizen?
By creation man was made in the class of God. Adam in the Garden of Eden, by God's wisdom, named all the animals one by one. But when he fell, his wisdom was corrupted. It did not take away the nature of God, yet the nature of the devil—sin and wickedness—spread to every mortal being through natural birth (John 3:5–6; Romans 3:23). But thank God for Jesus, who died for you and wants to spend eternity with you! You must accept that Jesus Christ is your Lord and Savior. Pray this prayer: “Lord Jesus, I am a sinner. Forgive me my sins. I know You died for me and rose on the third day. Wash me with Your blood. Come into my heart. I receive You today as my Lord and Savior. Write my name in the Book of Life. I am born again. I am now a Kingdom Citizen.”

Know Your Rights as a Kingdom Citizen

1. You Are a New Creation: 2 Corinthians 5:17 …

2. Your Sins Are Forgiven: Romans 8:1 …

3. Free from Curses: Galatians 3:13 …

4. Satan Is Under Your Feet: Ephesians 1:21 …

Know Your Duties as a Kingdom Citizen

You need knowledge—Hosea 4:6 … Attend foundation classes, study the Word (2 Timothy 2:15), read the Bible (2 Timothy 3:16), and do not miss church programmes (Hebrews 10:25). Get involved in church activities and join a service group.
