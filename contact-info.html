Get in Touch with Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L

We're here to help you connect with God and our church community. Whether you have questions, need prayer, want to join our fellowship, or simply need someone to talk to, we're just a call, message, or visit away.

At Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, we believe in being accessible to our members and visitors. Below you'll find all the ways you can reach us. Our team is ready to assist you with spiritual guidance, counseling, prayer requests, and information about our services and programs.

Feel free to contact us through any of the channels listed below. We look forward to hearing from you and welcoming you into our church family!
