Our Services

Join Us for Worship, Fellowship, and Spiritual Growth

At Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, we believe in creating an atmosphere where heaven touches earth. Our services are designed to equip, empower, and inspire you to live as a true Kingdom Citizen. Whether you're joining us for weekly worship, monthly gatherings, or annual conventions, you'll experience the power of God's presence and the love of a spiritual family.

We invite you to explore our service schedule and join us as we celebrate Jesus, grow in faith, and impact our community together. There's a place for you here!

Weekly Services

Sunday Services

Every Sunday

Sunday School: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Jesus Celebration Service: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Join us for powerful worship, inspiring messages, and fellowship. Our Sunday services are designed to refresh your spirit and equip you for the week ahead.

Wednesday Communion

Every Wednesday

Communion Service: 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM

A mid-week spiritual recharge featuring Holy Communion, healing prayers, and impactful teachings. This intimate service strengthens your walk with God.

Monthly Crossover

Last Day of Every Month

Crossover Service: 11:30 PM - 12:30 AM

Cross over into the new month with prayers, praise, and prophetic declarations. This powerful service sets the tone for blessings in the coming month.

Annual Programs

July

Judah Praise Festival

A month of explosive praise and worship where we lift up the name of Jesus. Experience God's presence through music, dance, and creative arts.

September

Youth Convention

An empowering gathering for young people featuring dynamic speakers, workshops, and networking opportunities. Equipping the next generation for kingdom impact.

October

Kingdom Harvest Time Convention

Our annual convention focused on spiritual growth, soul winning, and kingdom expansion. A time of refreshing, teaching, and harvest celebration.

Special Programs

Easter Love Feast

Celebrate the resurrection of Jesus Christ with special services, communion, and fellowship meals. A time of reflection, gratitude, and renewed hope.

December 7 Weekend Outreach

Our annual evangelism outreach where we take the love of Christ to the streets, prisons, hospitals, and communities. Sharing hope during the Christmas season.

Special Healing & Deliverance Services

Periodic services dedicated to prayer for healing, deliverance, and breakthrough. Experience God's miraculous power in your life and family.

Join Us This Week

Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, community, or a fresh encounter with God, there's a place for you at Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L. Our doors are open, and our hearts are ready to welcome you home.
