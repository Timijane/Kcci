Healing Miracle

HIV Positive to Negative!

March 15, 2023

A woman came to Pastor Sam Balogun with her medical test results confirming she was HIV 1 and 2 positive. With tears in her eyes, she showed the report to Pastor Sam, expecting prayer and sympathy.

Instead of praying, Pastor Sam took the test results and began to laugh! He quoted Psalm 2:4, "He who sits in the heavens laughs; the Lord holds them in derision."

Pastor Sam declared, "The King of kings is laughing at your sickness! This HIV certificate is now a certificate of healing!" He told her to go back for another test immediately.

The woman, though confused, obeyed in faith. To the amazement of the medical staff, her new test results came back completely negative! No trace of HIV 1 or 2 was found in her system.

Psalm 2:4 - "He who sits in the heavens laughs; the Lord holds them in derision."

Divine Healing

Sickness Flees Through Holy Dance

July 22, 2022

During one of our powerful church programs, a woman was brought in on a wheelchair. She was extremely sick and weak, holding onto her medical test results showing multiple serious conditions.

As Pastor Sam Balogun walked past her, the Holy Spirit prompted him to stop. He looked at her, saw the test results in her hand, and without saying a word, took her hand and began to dance with her!

At first, she could barely stand. But as Pastor Sam held her hand and danced with her, strength began to flow into her body. The more they danced, the stronger she became!

By the time the dance was over, she was dancing on her own with full strength. The sickness had completely left her body! She went back to the hospital the next day, and all tests came back normal. The doctors called it a medical miracle.

Psalm 30:11 - "You have turned my mourning into dancing..."

Marriage Miracle

From Single to Married in 3 Months!

November 8, 2023

A woman in her late 30s had been praying and waiting for a good husband for many years. She had attended countless prayer meetings, gone on many dates, but nothing worked out. She was beginning to lose hope.

She came to Pastor Sam Balogun expecting intense prayer and fasting. To her surprise, Pastor Sam said, "You don't need prayer for a husband. What you need is to stop praying and start acting!"

Pastor Sam gave her handbills for an upcoming church program and told her to go out and share them. "Your husband will find you while you're doing God's work," he declared.

As she was sharing the handbills at a marketplace, a godly businessman saw her and was struck by her dedication. He approached her, asked about the church, and started attending. Three months later, they were married! Today they serve together in ministry.

Proverbs 18:22 - "He who finds a wife finds a good thing and obtains favor from the Lord."

Financial Miracle

From Debt to Abundance

January 30, 2024

A brother was drowning in debt - over ₦5 million in loans with multiple creditors chasing him. His business had collapsed, and he was facing court cases and threats to his property.

Pastor Sam told him, "Your problem isn't money; it's your thinking. You're working for money instead of letting money work for you."

Pastor Sam taught him the principles of kingdom economics and gave him a specific seed to sow. The brother obeyed despite his situation and sowed his last ₦50,000.

Within one week, an old business partner called him with a contract worth ₦8 million! He was able to pay all his debts and still have ₦3 million left to restart his business. Today, he's debt-free and helping others get out of debt.

Malachi 3:10 - "Bring the full tithe... and see if I will not open the windows of heaven for you..."

Deliverance

Freedom from Spirit Husband

September 12, 2022

A young lady had been tormented by a spirit husband for 15 years. Every night, she would dream of being married to a strange man. In real life, every relationship failed, and she couldn't find peace.

During a deliverance service, Pastor Sam Balogun pointed at her and said, "You in the blue dress, your husband from the pit of hell is here with you today!"

The woman fell under the power of God. Pastor Sam commanded the spirit husband to manifest and leave. The demon screamed through her, "I've been married to her for 15 years!"

After intense warfare prayers, the demon was cast out. The woman testified that for the first time in 15 years, she slept peacefully without nightmares. Six months later, she met a godly man, and they are now happily married with a child.

Luke 10:19 - "I have given you authority... over all the power of the enemy..."

Fruitfulness

15 Years of Barrenness Broken

May 5, 2023

A couple had been married for 15 years without children. They had spent millions on fertility treatments, visited countless doctors, and even tried traditional remedies, all to no avail.

Pastor Sam Balogun told them, "Your womb isn't sick; it's under a curse. But today, that curse is broken!"

He placed his hand on the woman's womb and declared, "I command every spirit of barrenness to leave now! I speak fruitfulness and multiplication into this womb!"

That same month, the woman conceived naturally without any medical assistance! Nine months later, she gave birth to healthy twins - a boy and a girl. Today, they are proud parents of two beautiful children.

Exodus 23:26 - "None shall miscarry or be barren in your land..."
