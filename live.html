Join Our Online Worship Services & Sermons

Welcome to our online worship experience! Whether you're joining us from home, work, or anywhere in the world, we're excited to have you worship with us. At Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L, we believe in the power of God's presence, and we're committed to making His presence accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our live streaming services allow you to participate in worship, hear the Word of God, and connect with our church family in real-time. You can join us during our regular service times or watch recorded sermons at your convenience. No matter where you are, you're welcome here!

We stream our services live on multiple platforms to ensure you can join us from anywhere. Below you'll find links to our live streams, recent sermons, and ways to connect with our online community.
