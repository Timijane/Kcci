Christian Books & Resources for Spiritual Growth

Welcome to the Kingdom Citizens Church INT'L Online Library & Bookstore! We are delighted to offer you a carefully curated collection of Christian books written by our pastors and trusted Christian authors. Our mission is to provide spiritual nourishment, biblical teaching, and practical wisdom through quality Christian literature.

Browse our collection of books on Christian living, leadership, marriage, prayer, and spiritual growth. Each book has been selected to help you grow in your faith and deepen your relationship with God. You can preview excerpts, read reviews, and purchase books directly through our trusted partners.

Our library features exclusive works by Pastor Sam Balogun, Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun, Pastor Wale Somoye, and Pastor Peter Oluwakunbi. These anointed authors share insights from their ministry experience to equip and empower believers worldwide.
