About Foundation Class

The Foundation Class is a 12-week intensive discipleship program that covers the essential doctrines of the Christian faith. This course is mandatory for all new members and highly recommended for anyone wanting to deepen their understanding of biblical foundations.

Module 1: Salvation & New Birth

  • Understanding salvation
  • The new birth experience
  • Assurance of salvation
  • The finished work of Christ

Module 2: Water & Spirit Baptism

  • Biblical basis for baptism
  • Baptism of the Holy Spirit
  • Speaking in tongues
  • Gifts of the Spirit

Module 3: Church & Kingdom

  • Purpose of the church
  • Kingdom citizenship
  • Spiritual authority
  • Fivefold ministry

Module 4: Prayer & Warfare

  • Types of prayer
  • Spiritual warfare
  • Deliverance ministry
  • Maintaining victory
